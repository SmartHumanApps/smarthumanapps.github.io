The only instance in which we collect personal data is if you voluntarily communicate with us via email. In such cases, we may collect your:

We use this information solely for responding to your inquiry. We do not share, sell, or use this information for any other purpose.

We retain emails only for as long as necessary to respond to user inquiries. If you wish to have your email correspondence deleted, you may contact us at contact us, and we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within a few business days.

Under applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data

Since we do not collect personal data, the exercise of these rights is limited to email correspondence.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers in our Apps or on our website.

We take appropriate security measures to protect the limited data we may process (email communications). However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Apps and website do not integrate third-party services that collect personal data, such as Google Analytics, Firebase, AdMob, or social media plugins.

Our Apps and website are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will delete it.
